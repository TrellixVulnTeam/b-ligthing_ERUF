Our company was founded in 2010 by the Afonso family in a small island in the Atlantic Ocean. Our natural surroundings and beautiful scenery are our main inspiration. Handcrafted lamps with precious materials make our creative process an elaborate dance between designers and artisans. Our vision is to create one of a kind statement pieces which upgrade any space.

Every day we search for new and better lighting solutions to empower the designs with the best lighting instruments to orchestrate high-end items. Each detail contributes to the whole picture and even unnoticed, our luminaires play a significant role. Changing the perception or ruling the atmosphere, pure design and simplicity make them discreet or outstanding, yet effective.
