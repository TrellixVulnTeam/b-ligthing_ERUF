ISRAEL

Our showroom in Israel is located in the vibrant and bustling neighboorhood of Neve Tzedek. On the second floor in a Bauhaus building you can find an array of our lamps and lighting in a very futuristic and envolving display. The prestigious interior designer Nate Berkus has been in charge of our showroom since 2018, creating avant-garde and spectacular spaces using our one of a kind pieces

DUBAI

Dubai is a country of traditions but it looks forward into the future. Our showroom is located in the historic part of the town, in a charming and traditional street. B-Lighting signature lamps contrast with the arab and baroque elements, giving an eclectic twist which blends into perfection. The organic forms of our lighting are strategically placed by Nur Ahmed, a local interior designer whose vision has taken our brand further in the Arabian Gulf
